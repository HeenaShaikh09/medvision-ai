Privacy & security
Your data is yours alone

Built on Supabase with database-level Row Level Security — no one can accidentally see another patient's data.

  • You only ever see your own health records, reports, and appointments.
  • Your AI risk score is read-only — only your doctor can change or override it.
  • Sessions use JWT tokens and expire automatically for your safety.
  • Academic project — no ads, no data sold, privacy by design.
FAQ
Common questions
How do I create a patient account?+
Go to the Patient Portal at localhost:5173/patient-login and click the Register tab. Fill in your email, create a password, enter your display name and date of birth. Your account is ready immediately.
When will my AI risk score appear?+
Your AI risk score is calculated automatically once a hospital admin or doctor adds your health record — including your diagnosis, age, and symptom flags. Once a doctor verifies the report, it becomes visible in your My Health section.
Can I change my own risk score?+
No — your AI risk score is read-only for patients. Only your assigned doctor can review, verify, or override it. This ensures clinical accuracy and a full audit trail. You can log daily symptoms which inform your doctor's review.
How do I download my health report?+
Go to My Reports in the patient portal. Any report marked as "AI Verified" has a PDF download button. The PDF includes your risk score, symptom breakdown, doctor notes, and appointment history.
Is this system free to use?+
Yes — MedVision AI is a 100% open-source academic project from Jamia Hamdard (SEST 2024–26). There is no cost for patients to register and use the portal. Built entirely on open-source technologies with no licensing fees.
How is my data kept private?+
MedVision AI uses Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) — a database-level policy ensuring every user can only access their own data. Admins, doctors, and patients all have completely separate, isolated data views. Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth with JWT tokens.
